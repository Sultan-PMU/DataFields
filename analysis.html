📊 Domain Overview

Data Analysis is the practice of examining raw datasets to find meaningful patterns and trends. Analysts act as translators between complex data and business stakeholders. By cleaning data and building interactive dashboards, they answer critical business questions. This field is ideal for logical thinkers who enjoy statistics, visual storytelling, and solving real-world business puzzles using structured data tools.
