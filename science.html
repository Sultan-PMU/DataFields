🔬 Domain Overview

Data Science combines mathematics, advanced programming, and domain expertise to build predictive models. Unlike analysts who look backward, data scientists look forward using Machine Learning algorithms. They train computer systems to recognize complex data patterns and automate future decisions. It is a deeply technical and exciting field perfect for those passionate about algorithms, artificial intelligence, and deep computing math.
