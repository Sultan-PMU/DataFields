💡 Domain Overview

Business Intelligence (BI) focuses on the practical application of data within corporate strategies. BI specialists design structured semantic data layers, maintain enterprise warehouses, and deploy rich corporate reporting solutions. While analysts explore raw datasets, BI experts build permanent, highly reliable tracking systems that executives use daily to monitor KPIs. It is a fantastic career choice for tech-savvy individuals who enjoy corporate strategy, reporting architecture, and executive-level visual storytelling.
