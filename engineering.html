⚙️ Domain Overview

Data Engineering is the backbone of the entire data lifecycle. Engineers focus on designing, building, and maintaining the vital pipelines that transport data safely. They transform messy, raw systems into highly structured datasets ready for analysis. If you enjoy software engineering, cloud architecture, system design, and managing huge streams of enterprise database systems, this technical field is for you.
