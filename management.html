🛡️ Domain Overview

Data Management covers the strategic policies, governance frameworks, and security measures needed to protect institutional data assets. Professionals in this track oversee data quality, metadata, master data, and official compliance standards. They ensure that an organization's databases remain safe, fully trusted, legal, and operational. It is an ideal path for individuals interested in data policy, security infrastructure, and enterprise system compliance.
